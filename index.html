Welcome to PM Workload Tracker

    
A quick tour to get you started — takes 30 seconds.


    

      
Start with Your BigTime Allocations

      
The fastest way to set up is to import your BigTime allocation spreadsheet. This creates your project codes, sub-codes, and monthly hour budgets automatically.

      
📊In BigTime, the Job column becomes your project (parent code), and the Task column becomes your sub-code.

      
💡You can import anytime from the Allocations tab. Re-importing updates current and future months without touching past data.

      

        
        or skip and set up manually later
      

    


    

      
Your 5 Tabs

      
1My Tasks — your personal to-do list with timers, priorities, and recurring tasks.

      
2Projects — organize work into projects with sessions and subtasks.

      
3Team Deliverables — track cross-team assignments and delegated work.

      
4Timesheet — pay-period and monthly views of logged vs planned hours.

      
5Allocations — monthly capacity planning by person and project.

    


    

      
Adding Tasks & Tracking Time

      
Use + Add Task or press N to create a task. Set a priority, due date, and time estimate.

      
⏱Click the play button on any task to start a timer. When you complete the task, actual hours are logged to your timesheet automatically.

      
↻Tasks can recur weekly, biweekly, or monthly — set it up in the task edit modal.

    


    

      
Projects & Sessions

      
The Projects tab groups work into projects. Each project can have multiple sessions (work items) and subtasks.

      
📂Sessions have their own timers, due dates, and can be delegated to team members.

      
      
    


    

      
Keyboard Shortcuts & Tips

      
⌨N = new task   1-5 = switch tabs   / = search   Esc = close modal   Ctrl+Z = undo delete

      
☁Your data syncs to the cloud automatically. Click the cloud icon in the header to force a sync.

      
ℹClick the (i) button in the bottom-right corner anytime for help, tips, and shortcuts.
